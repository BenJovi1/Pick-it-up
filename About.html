About the creator

My name is Ben Weaver, I am a self taught guitar player and have been playing for about 3 years now.
I created this website to help beginners learn how to play the guitar in a simple and easy to
understand way for a 9th grade school project. I hope you found the information on this website helpful
and that it inspires you to pick up the guitar and start playing!

Purpose of the website

The purpose of this website is to provide a comprehensive guide for beginners who want to learn how to play the guitar.
The website covers essential chords, tabs, and advanced techniques to help beginners improve their skills and
become better guitar players.

How i learned guitar

I learned how to play guitar through a combination of online resources, YouTube tutorials, and practice.
I started with basic chords and strumming patterns and gradually worked my way up to more advanced techniques.
Consistent practice and dedication were key to my progress, and I encourage beginners to stay motivated and keep practicing.
